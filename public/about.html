Our Story

How We Started

Travor was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make travel accessible, enjoyable, and memorable for everyone. What started as a small team of passionate travelers has grown into a global travel agency with a presence in over 20 countries.

Our journey began when our founders, experienced travelers themselves, recognized the need for a travel agency that truly understood the needs of modern travelers. They envisioned a company that would combine personalized service with cutting-edge technology to create seamless travel experiences.

Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of travelers explore the world, create lasting memories, and discover new cultures. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and passion for travel continues to drive everything we do.

Our Mission

What Drives Us

At Travor, our mission is to inspire and enable people to explore the world, experience different cultures, and create meaningful connections through travel. We believe that travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and foster understanding between people of different backgrounds.
